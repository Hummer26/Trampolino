A modern, visually impressive, responsive **landing page / business-card website**.

## Main goal
The main goal is to make the visitor contact the company:

- by phone;
- by WhatsApp;
- by email;
- through a contact form;
- through Facebook.

## Business summary
Salto Trampolino provides bungee trampoline attractions and inflatable castles for children, families, private events, public events, school events, corporate events and festivals across Switzerland.

The business offers:

1. Bungee trampoline attractions at service points or events.
2. Inflatable castles and inflatable attractions for children.
3. Equipment and a dedicated operator/person who manages the attraction during custom events.

## Core positioning
Salto Trampolino brings safe, active and memorable entertainment to parties and events across Switzerland.

## What must NOT be shown
Do not show:

- fixed prices;
- fixed opening hours;
- fixed permanent locations;
- a complex booking calendar;
- online payment;
- a customer account;
- misleading promises like “always available everywhere”.

## What must be shown instead
Use flexible wording:

- “Operativi in tutta la Svizzera”
- “Disponibilità su richiesta”
- “Contattaci per informazioni e preventivi”
- “Le località e gli orari possono variare in base alla stagione, agli eventi e alle richieste.”

## Confirmed services

### 1. Bungee Trampoline / Trampolini con elastici
A trampoline attraction with elastic cords where children and adults can jump safely and experience a flying sensation.

Important facts to include:

- professional bungee trampoline structures;
- Swiss-made structures according to supplied client text;
- European TÜV certification according to supplied client text;
- regular checks and tests;
- jump height up to approximately 9 meters;
- user weight range approximately 15–85 kg;
- available for events and collaborations across Switzerland.

### 2. Inflatable castles / Castelli gonfiabili
Colorful inflatable attractions for children.

Important facts to include:

- suitable approximately for children from 3 to 12 years;
- safe, soft, colorful and fun;
- suitable for birthdays, family parties, corporate events, outdoor events;
- include slides, large play surfaces and soft inflatable walls.

### 3. Events with equipment and operator
The company can provide both the equipment and a person/operator who manages the attraction during the event.

This is a major selling point because the customer does not need to manage the technical or operational side alone.
